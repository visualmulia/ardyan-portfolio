Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you calculate the project pricing?

Pricing is based on estimated complexity, base technology (Landing page vs native App vs full-stack SaaS), and the specific integrations required (APIs, payment setup, databases). You can get an initial range using our interactive configurator above.

What is the typical project development timeline?

Simple landing pages are typically delivered within 1 week. Custom web applications, native Android apps, and SaaS dashboards generally range from 3 to 8 weeks depending on the size of the feature scope.

Do you offer revisions and ongoing maintenance?

Yes. All contracts include a standard 14-day QA window with unlimited iterations to fix layout issues. I also offer monthly support SLA retainers to update features, backup databases, and patch dependencies.

What are your primary communication channels?

I maintain transparent client communication. My active channels are WhatsApp (for quick chats and status updates), Email (for official proposals and files), and Slack/Discord (for developer collaboration pipelines).

How do you guarantee 99.9% uptime for scrapers?

I host automations on virtual private servers (VPS) managed by robust Docker containerization. Scrapers utilize automatic IP rotations via proxy services to prevent bans, and integrate heartbeat monitoring APIs to auto-restart and alert on errors.
